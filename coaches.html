Meet Our Coach

My passion, love, and drive for volleyball started when I was age 4 watching my mother play. The moment I was allowed on the court to huddle up with her and her teammates, I knew I wanted to be a volleyball player.


My career officially began at age 13, advancing through club, high school, and college. I graduated from Connellsville High School in 2008, where I was a 4-year varsity starter, team leader, 3x All-Section, All Conference, and All WPIAL 3rd Team as well as leading our team to a Section 2 undefeated championship season.

Coach Jess Logan


I led Penn Highlands VBC to be their only team to have ever won the OVR Championship in 2013. We beat out a field of 193 of the areas best teams to take home the title and competed at the National level for the only time in the team's history.
I progressed to the collegiate level at Seton Hill University, later transferring to Bethany College to finish my career. During my time at Bethany, I was able to assist my team in winning ECAC Championships in back to back years (2015 & 2016).
